Baguette

Description

Baguette is a type of French bread made with flour, water, yeast, and salt. It has a long, narrow shape, crispy crust, and soft interior. It's often used as a sandwich bread and is popular all over the world.

Brioche

Description

Brioche is a French bread enriched with butter and eggs, giving it a fluffy texture and sweet flavor. It's used to make various pastries and desserts, and is considered a luxurious bread.

Ciabatta

Description

Ciabatta is an Italian bread with a crispy crust, soft interior, and large air pockets. It's made with a simple dough and often used for sandwiches or as a side bread.

Focaccia

Description

Focaccia is a soft, Italian flatbread made with flour, yeast, water, and olive oil. It's often flavored with herbs and toppings and served as a side dish or appetizer.

Pita Bread

Description

Pita bread is a Middle Eastern flatbread with a pocket in the center that's filled with ingredients like falafel or hummus. It's baked at a high temperature and served as a wrap or with dips.

Rye Bread

Description

Rye bread is a dense, earthy-flavored bread made from rye flour. It's popular in Northern and Eastern Europe, often used for sandwiches and has health benefits due to its high fiber content.

Sour Dough Bread

Description

Sourdough bread is made using a natural fermentation process that gives it a tangy flavor and chewy texture. It's typically made with wheat and rye flours and used for toast, sandwiches, or served alongside meals. It has a long shelf life and is known for its nutritional benefits.

Whold Wheat Bread

Description

Whole wheat bread is made from whole wheat flour that includes the bran and germ, giving it a nuttier flavor and denser texture. It's often used for sandwiches or toast, and is a healthier alternative to white bread due to its higher fiber and nutrient content.
