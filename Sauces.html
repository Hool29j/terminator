Sauces

Unagi Sauce

Description

Unagi sauce is a sweet and savory Japanese sauce made from soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. It is commonly used as a condiment for grilled eel dishes and as a dipping sauce for other foods. It is a popular condiment in Japanese cuisine.

Avocado Ranch

Description

Avocado ranch is a creamy and tangy sauce made by combining ranch dressing with mashed avocado. It is commonly used as a dip or a salad dressing in American cuisine.

Béarnaise Sauce

Description

Béarnaise sauce is a classic French sauce made with clarified butter, egg yolks, white wine vinegar, shallots, and herbs. It has a rich and tangy flavor and is commonly served with grilled meats or vegetables.

Béchamel Sauce

Description

Béchamel sauce is a French sauce made from a roux of flour and butter, and milk. It's a creamy and versatile sauce that can be used as a base for many other sauces or as a component in dishes like lasagna or mac and cheese. It's a fundamental sauce in French cuisine and used worldwide.

Cocktail Sauce

Description

Cocktail sauce is a cold, pink sauce made from ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce. It's commonly used as a dip for shrimp and other seafood and has a tangy and slightly spicy taste. Cocktail sauce is a classic American condiment.

Espagnole Sauce

Description

Espagnole sauce, also known as brown sauce, is a classic French sauce made by simmering beef or veal bones, vegetables, and aromatics with tomato paste and red wine. It's then thickened with a roux and used as a base for other sauces or in dishes like stews or braised meats. It's a fundamental sauce in French cuisine and used worldwide.

Hollandaises Sauce

Description

Hollandaise sauce is a classic French sauce made from egg yolks, butter, lemon juice, and cayenne pepper. It has a rich, buttery taste and pairs well with dishes like eggs Benedict or steamed vegetables. It's a delicate sauce that requires careful attention to temperature and technique.

Velouté Sauce

Description

Velouté sauce is a French sauce made from a roux and a light-colored stock, such as chicken, veal, or fish stock. It has a smooth and velvety texture and a delicate flavor. It's a fundamental sauce in French cuisine and is used as a base for other sauces or in dishes like pot pies and soups.
