Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are stuffed eggs which are split in half which are hard-boiled. They are originated from Rome. It is filled with paste made from egg yolks mixed together with other ingredients like mayonnaise and mustard.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Paprika, for garnish

Step By Step

  1. Place the eggs in a single layer in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the water is boiling, remove the pan from the heat, cover with a lid and let sit for 12 minutes.
  3. After 12 minutes, transfer the eggs to a bowl of ice water to cool for at least 5 minutes.
  4. Once the eggs have cooled, carefully peel them and slice in half lengthwise.
  5. Remove the yolks from the eggs and place them in a bowl. Mash the yolks with a fork.
  6. Add mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper to the yolks and stir to combine.
  7. Using a spoon, carefully fill each egg white half with the yolk mixture.
  8. Sprinkle with paprika for garnish.
  9. Serve chilled.
