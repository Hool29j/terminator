Chicken Wings

Chicken wings are a popular appetizer made from the chicken wing drumettes and flats, which are typically fried, baked, or grilled until crispy and golden brown. Chicken wings can be served plain or coated in a variety of sauces, such as buffalo sauce, barbecue sauce, honey garlic sauce, or teriyaki sauce

They can be served with a variety of dipping sauces, such as blue cheese or ranch dressing, and are often accompanied by celery sticks and carrot sticks. Chicken wings are a popular dish for sports events, parties, and casual dining establishments, and are enjoyed by many people around the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Step By Step

  1. 1.Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack on top of the foil.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels.
  5. Place the chicken wings in a large mixing bowl, add the olive oil, and toss to coat.
  6. Sprinkle the seasoning mixture over the chicken wings, and toss until evenly coated.
  7. Place the chicken wings on the wire rack, making sure they are not touching each other.
  8. Bake the chicken wings for 40-45 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and golden brown.
  9. Remove the chicken wings from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.
