Caprese Salad

Caprese salad is a simple Italian salad that typically consists of sliced fresh tomatoes, sliced fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. The salad is often drizzled with olive oil and seasoned with salt and pepper.

The colors of the salad (red, white, and green) are said to resemble the colors of the Italian flag, which has contributed to its popularity in Italian cuisine. Caprese salad is a light and refreshing dish that is often served as an appetizer or side dish in Italy and around the world.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Step By Step

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into equally sized pieces.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving dish, alternating between them.
  3. Sprinkle the chopped basil leaves over the top of the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar until well combined.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the top of the Caprese salad.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper, to taste.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
