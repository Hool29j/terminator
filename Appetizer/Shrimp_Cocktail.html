Shrimp Cocktail

Shrimp cocktail is a classic appetizer that typically consists of chilled, cooked shrimp that are served with a tangy, tomato-based cocktail sauce. The cocktail sauce is often made with ketchup, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and hot sauce, and can be adjusted to taste with additional spices and seasonings.

Shrimp cocktail is typically served on a bed of lettuce or cabbage, and may be garnished with lemon wedges or fresh herbs. It is a popular dish for special occasions and events, such as weddings, cocktail parties, and holiday gatherings, and is enjoyed by many seafood lovers around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup prepared horseradish
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges and fresh parsley for garnish

Step By Step

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the ketchup, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, hot sauce, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Cover the bowl and chill the sauce in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  3. Arrange the cooked shrimp on a platter and place the bowl of cocktail sauce in the center.
  4. Garnish with lemon wedges and fresh parsley
  5. Serve immediately, with the shrimp dipped in the cocktail sauce.
