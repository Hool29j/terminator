Eggplant Parmesan

Eggplant Parmesan, also known as Parmigiana di melanzane, is a classic Italian dish that features sliced and breaded eggplant that is layered with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese, and then baked until golden and bubbly.

The dish is typically served hot and is a favorite among vegetarians and meat-lovers alike due to its rich, hearty flavor and satisfying texture. Eggplant Parmesan is a popular dish in Italian-American cuisine and is often served as a main course accompanied by a side of pasta or a fresh salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 3 cups Italian-style breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 4 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Step By Step

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sprinkle the eggplant slices with salt and let them sit for 30 minutes to release excess water. Pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. Set up three shallow bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, and chopped basil.
  4. Dredge each eggplant slice in flour, then dip it into the egg mixture, and coat it with the breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the eggplant slices in batches until they are golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side. Drain them on paper towels.
  6. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of fried eggplant slices on top of the sauce, then cover them with more sauce and a sprinkling of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layering until all of the eggplant slices are used up.
  7. Bake the eggplant Parmesan for 30-35 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the sauce is hot and bubbly.
  8. Let the eggplant Parmesan cool for 5-10 minutes before serving. Serve with additional grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil, if desired.
