Vegetable Lasagna

Vegetable lasagna is a healthy and delicious variation of the classic Italian pasta dish. It features layers of lasagna noodles, cheese, and a variety of cooked vegetables, such as zucchini, eggplant, bell peppers, and spinach.

The dish is often made with a creamy ricotta and/or mozzarella cheese mixture and can be seasoned with herbs and spices like basil and garlic. Vegetable lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that can be served as a main course or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 yellow squash, chopped
  • 1 yellow squash, chopped
  • 1 eggplant, chopped
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Step By Step

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, and eggplant to the pan. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pan. Simmer for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, followed by a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture and a layer of the vegetable mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of lasagna noodles on top.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
