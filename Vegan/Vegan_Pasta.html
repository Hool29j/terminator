Vegan Pasta

Vegan pasta is a type of pasta that is made without any animal products, such as eggs or dairy. It's typically made from durum wheat semolina, water, and sometimes other plant-based ingredients like spinach or tomato. Vegan pasta can be found in a variety of shapes and forms, including spaghetti, linguine, penne, and fusilli.

It can be served with a variety of sauces and toppings, including tomato sauce, pesto, or roasted vegetables. Vegan pasta is a popular alternative for those following a vegan or vegetarian diet, or for anyone looking to reduce their consumption of animal products.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. dried pasta
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Vegan parmesan cheese (optional)

Step By Step

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes, until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the chopped red bell pepper and sauté for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with vegan parmesan cheese if desired.
