Vegan Pizza

Vegan pizza is a pizza that is made without any animal products, such as cheese or meat. The crust is typically made with flour, water, yeast, and olive oil, and is topped with tomato sauce, vegetables like mushrooms, onions, and peppers, and sometimes plant-based cheese made from nuts or soy.

Other toppings like tofu, tempeh, or seitan can also be added to increase the protein content. Vegan pizza is a popular option for those following a vegan or vegetarian diet, or for anyone looking to reduce their consumption of animal products.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Pizza Toppings

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup vegan cheese (optional)

Step By Step

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the active dry yeast, sugar, and warm water. Set aside for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, and mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until the dough has doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to the desired thickness and shape. Place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edges.
  7. Add the sliced red onion, red bell pepper, mushrooms, and black olives on top of the sauce.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven, sprinkle with fresh basil, and vegan cheese (if using). Serve hot.
