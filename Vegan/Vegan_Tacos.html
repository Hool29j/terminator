Vegan Tacos

Vegan tacos are tacos that are made without any animal products, such as meat or dairy. The filling is typically made with plant-based ingredients such as beans, tofu, tempeh, or seitan, along with vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and peppers. The taco shells can be made from corn or flour and are usually heated or fried until crispy.

Vegan tacos can be seasoned with a variety of spices, such as cumin, chili powder, or paprika, and can be topped with guacamole, salsa, or vegan sour cream. Vegan tacos are a popular choice for those following a vegan or vegetarian diet, or for anyone looking to reduce their consumption of animal products.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup of corn
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6-8 corn tortillas
  • Toppings (optional): diced avocado, sliced jalapeños, chopped cilantro, salsa, hot sauce

Step By Step

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil, onion, and red bell pepper. Cook until the vegetables are soft and fragrant, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the drained and rinsed black beans and corn to the skillet. Stir to combine and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Add the chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine and cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  5. Heat the corn tortillas in the oven for 5 minutes, until they are warm and soft.
  6. Fill each tortilla with a generous spoonful of the black bean and corn mixture.
  7. Top with your desired toppings, such as diced avocado, sliced jalapeños, chopped cilantro, salsa, or hot sauce.
  8. Serve and enjoy your vegan tacos!

NUTRITION FACTS

Fat : 5-7 grams per taco (mostly from the olive oil and avocado, if used as a topping)

Protein: about 6-8 grams per taco (mostly from the black beans)

Sodium: 580mg

Carbohydrates: 30-35 grams per taco (mostly from the corn tortilla and black bean and corn mixture)

Fiber: 6-8 grams per taco (mostly from the black beans and corn)

Vitamins and minerals: vegan tacos are likely to be a good source of vitamin C, folate, iron, and magnesium, depending on the ingredients used.
